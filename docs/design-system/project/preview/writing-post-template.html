Notes on Drake export, again

A short follow-up after a second tax season of watching one CPA firm wrestle a specific export into a shape that's actually reviewable.

Export is harder than the import

The temptation, when you're building one of these tools, is to put all of your effort into the part where the data comes in.

Key takeaway
The part that decides whether the tool gets used is the export.

I now spend roughly half of any small-tool budget on the export surface. The boring half.
