Note
Drake exports tab-delimited by default. Set the delimiter in the export config, not the import.
Key takeaway
The export is harder than the import.
Watch out
Column order changes year to year — version it once, near the top of your script.
Tip
A weekly email with the day's stamps as a CSV attachment is the simplest export surface that works.
TL;DR
Spend half your budget on export. The rest is engineering shaped, but export is what gets used.
